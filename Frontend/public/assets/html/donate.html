Make a Donation

Your support makes the difference in what we can do and how many animals we can rescue and rehabilitate.

Your kind donation will go towards providing short and long term care for the HOS rescued animals.

But your donation is also spreading much further and into the community. Our community outreach program is an essential part of our mission, providing life changing rehabilitation's to neglected strays, but also providing hope, support and education to local poverty-stricken Cambodian families.

legacy donation

Giving Aloha Ilio a legacy gift, means allocating a donation to be paid to our nonprofit after your passing. We deeply appreciate that you have thought of us in your planning.

The work never stops here, and allocating donations well into the future is one of the best ways for us to plan.

To get the information you need for a legacy donation, please fill in the contact form below and one of our staff will be in touch as soon as possible.

Once again, thank you for considering Aloha Ilio in your estate planning.
