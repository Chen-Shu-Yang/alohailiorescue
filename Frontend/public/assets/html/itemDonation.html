Item Donation

Our donation drop-off address is:
R & D Equipment Repair, 16-579 Kipimana st, Keaau HI. 96749

Any gift cards or small, mailable items can be sent to our PO Box:
PO Box 492364 Keaau. HI. 96749

Food donation

We happily accept food donations to our drop off point at:
R & D Equipment Repair, 16-579 Kipimana st, Keaau HI. 96749

but we ask that you purchase the brand we prefer, as it is easy on all the dog's tummies and prevents us changing diet for them frequently. We feed Kirkland Signature Dog Food Variety (Chicken, Rice, and Vegetable Formula).

3 ways to buy us food

Shop
You can find this food at Ohana Foods, Cost-U-Less in Hilo, and Dimple Cheek in Mountain View (see map on right). It can also be purchased from Costco in Kona.


Online
If you prefer, this food can be ordered online on Amazon or via the Costco Website.

Gift Cards
If you would like to send either an Amazon gift card or a Costco gift card designated for food please mail the gift card to Aloha Ilio Rescue, PO Box 492364 Keaau. HI. 96749 or email a digital card to daylynn@alohailiorescue.com with a note specifying it should be spent on food.

PLEASE do not mail us food, either use the drop off or gift card options.
